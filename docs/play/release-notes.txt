Scan screen improvements from tester feedback:

- More packs are recognized as a BMS without turning on "show all devices":
  LLT Power names, bare module model codes (SP04S034, AP21S002, ...), and
  any device advertising the JBD Bluetooth service, whatever its name.
- The device list no longer reorders with every signal-strength change and
  briefly-quiet devices no longer vanish and reappear, so the row you are
  aiming for stays under your finger.
